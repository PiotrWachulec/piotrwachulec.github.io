Hi!

From the 1st of March 2024, I’ve started a new journey - #100Commitow challenge. It’s a challenge to develop an open-source project constantly over 100 days. I will work on a project called ‘VM Start/Stop’. I’m going to share my progress on my YouTube channel. I’m going to share my thoughts, my struggles, and my successes. I’m going to share my knowledge and experience. I’m going to share my journey with you!

On the challenge page, you can find more details about the challenge, the project, and the rules: 100commitow. You can also find the links to the repositories with the other projects.

And, of course, you can see my repository here: @PiotrWachulec/vm-start-stop.

Feel free to make PRs, issues, forks, comments, whatever! I’m open to any feedback and help!
